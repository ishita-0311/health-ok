Health-OK Project - Quick References

Supported Diseases and Kaggle Datasets:
1. Heart Disease Prediction
   - Dataset: Cleveland Heart Disease Dataset
   - Key Models: Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost
2. Diabetes Risk Assessment
   - Dataset: Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset
   - Key Models: SVM, KNN, MLP
3. Breast Cancer Classification
   - Dataset: Breast Cancer Wisconsin (Diagnostic) Dataset
   - Key Models: SVC, Random Forest, ANN
4. Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)
   - Dataset: Chronic Kidney Disease Dataset
   - Key Models: Decision Trees, LightGBM
